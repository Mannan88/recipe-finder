A pancake, also known as hotcake, griddlecake, or flapjack, is a flat cake, often thin and round, prepared from a starch-based batter that may contain berries, milk, and butter, and then cooked on a hot surface such as a griddle or frying pan. It is a type of batter bread

Ingredients

  • flour
  • 1/2 tbsp butter
  • olive oil (optional)
  • milk
  • sugar
  • salt
